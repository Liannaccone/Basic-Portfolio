About Me

I graduated from Georgetown University in 2014 with a BS in Healthcare Management and Policy. For the past three years, I worked at Triage Consulting Group as a Senior Associate leading teams of consultants providing payer strategy and revenue cycle consulting services to health systems throughout the country. The technical skills I gained manipulating and managing large sets of data fueled my interest in software engineering.


I am currently attending UC Berkeley's Coding School and am excited to explore software engineering-related opportunities in the healthcare industry in the upcoming weeks.
